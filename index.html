About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my site!

I'm a passionate game enthusiast eager to break into the industry and make a lasting impact.

My journey into gaming has a unique story—when I was younger, I had a special eye condition, and the doctor advised my parents to let me exercise my eyes more. That's how I got my first Game Boy and PC, and I've been hooked ever since!

Currently, I'm playing Deadlock by Valve, which I believe is the ultimate evolution of hero shooter games.
