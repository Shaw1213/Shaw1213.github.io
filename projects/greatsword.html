Greatsword Combat System

Dark Souls-inspired combat demo. 6 attack types, HitStop + CameraShake, Behavior Tree AI with stagger/knockback, GAS attributes. Built in 7 days.

Combat Architecture

  • 6 attack types: light, heavy, sprint, shield bash, charged, jump attack
  • Each attack has independent damage values and stamina cost
  • Stamina management creates high-risk/high-reward combat rhythm

Hit Feedback

  • HitStop + CameraShake scaled by attack type (light 1.0x, heavy 2.0x, charged 3.0x)
  • Frame freeze and camera shake emphasize the weight of the greatsword

Enemy AI & Reaction System

  • Behavior Tree-driven AI
  • Three reaction types: stagger, knockback, stun
  • GameplayTag-driven reaction selection for differentiated combat feedback

Resource & State Management

  • Stamina as core combat resource: attacks, rolls, sprints all consume stamina
  • Delayed recovery forces meaningful attack/defend decisions
  • Roll, dodge, block, light/heavy hit differentiation via Rising/Falling edge detection
